BIO

Hello, my name is Alexander and I am a Front-end developer. I live in Enschede and I enjoy learning new technologies in general. The skills that I have are HTML, CSS, Javascript, Vue.js, Sass and Wordpress. I also have some experience in UI/UX and I use Figma & sometimes Adobe Illustrator for tooling. I also have knowlege in some back-end development since I worked with C#, PHP and MySQL(For databases). I am always open for new challenges and learning in general.

Recreate B.V.

Front-end Web Developer (Internship)
HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP, MySQL

In Recreate not only have I learned alot about front-end development, but also about working in Agile and Git. I have also learned how to plan my work effectively and to make the most out of my time there. My job was to make a Dashboard for customer of Recreate to show their progress in a project. I was in a team of three people, with me as the front-end and two people at back-end. My responsibility was to make the UI and overall design of the website and I quite enjoyed my experience at Recreate in general. I have learned a lot about the basics of CSS and JavaScript there and also how to work in a team.

MediaFris

Front-end Web Developer (Internship)
HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP, MySQL, Wordpress

In this small startup company, I have created many things. From small features on a website to creating a complete Netflix-like website. The project I was working on is called VolKoren.tv and it was already an on going project for over a year. That was also my exam-assignment to graduate my MBO Level 4 program as an software engineer. Unlike Netflix with movies, the project was about making a website for customers to watch different kinds of choir performances. It could be from classic, romance or church choir to modern, pop and acapella groups from all over the Netherlands. The website is made with Wordpress, but the theme that is used there is created by me. Most of my work has been put into the theme development and my biggest challenge was to make it work for all devices.
